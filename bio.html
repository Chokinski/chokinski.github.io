About Me

Aidan Korczynski is an undergraduate 3rd year computer science student at Southern Connecticut State University in New Haven. Aspiring and pursuing a career in full-stack software development and making use of Machine Learning, Aidan's main interests are in creating Decentralized Software while dipping his toes into the rapidly advancing field of Artificial Intelligence.


Hobbies

In my free time, I'm usually socializing with friends and family, watching black mirror, or working on side projects to build upon my general knowledge and skillset.


Childhood

Born in Georgia yet raised in Willimantic, CT, I've been around mostly middleclass and lowerclass folk. As a child, my love for the computer science field developed from playing games on our dell all-in-one desktop computer, with one of the main games being Minecraft still in it's alpha stage. Since then, I've shifted from wanting to be a professional gamer or streamer as a kid, to (as cliche as it sounds) wanting to produce the systems and software that help push humanity's technology forward.
